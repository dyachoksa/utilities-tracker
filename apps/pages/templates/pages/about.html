{% blocktrans %}The Utilities Tracker is a small non-profit project for tracking utility usage and getting basic consumption statistics.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Do you find it tedious to keep track of your gas, water, and electricity usage every month? Do you often forget the numbers from the previous month and have to start over? We understand how irritating this can be, so we've created a solution that simplifies the process for you. Our user-friendly system allows you to easily record and access your usage data without the hassle of pen and paper. Give it a try and let us know what you think!{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}What we can: provide you a way to store information about your homes, utility providers and usage records.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}What we can't: automate the payment process or sync payment plans from your provider.{% endblocktrans %}
